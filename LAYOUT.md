## Color Scheme
- Background: Dark gray (#0f172a)
- Cards: Slightly lighter gray (#1e293b)
- Primary accent: Blue (#3b82f6)
- Text: White/Light gray
- Borders: Subtle gray (#334155)

## Spacing
- Max width: 1200px (centered)
- Section padding: 80px vertical
- Card gaps: 24px
- Border radius: 12px

## Typography
- Headings: Bold, large (48px hero, 36px section)
- Body: Regular, readable (18px)
- Emojis: 48px for feature icons
